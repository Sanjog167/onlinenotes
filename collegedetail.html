Softwarica College is recognized as a high-quality academic institution that provides range of bachelor programs. It is located among the busiest place of Kathmandu, Purano Baneshwor with diverse society where college can include students from every corner of Nepal. Thames International College consists of two schools based on field of study, School of Business and Technology and School of Social Sciences. School of Business and Technology currently provides Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), Bachelor of Business Management (BBM), Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) and Bachelor of Information Management (BIM), Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA). School of Social Sciences offers BA courses which includes Bachelor of Social work, Bachelor of Rural Development, Bachelor of Journalism and Mass Communication, Bachelor of Sociology and Bachelor of Psychology. Thames International College has gained reputation from not only through its education excellence but also through full dedication from experienced academicians and excellent learning environment. Thames International College has been focusing on how to prepare students to deal with the real-world challenges and facilitate students on how to independently make appropriate choice. Special class rooms are configured to enhance interactive learning for students.
